Practical Information

Venue & Lunch

The workshop takes place at the Palau de Congressos de Palma, co-located with LREC 2026. There is no organized conference lunch. Restaurants and the beach are within walking distance.

Poster Presentations

  • Poster format: A0, Portrait orientation (see LREC Authors Kit).
  • All poster sessions share a common exhibition area at the Palau de Congressos.
  • Poster boards remain available before and after the coffee break within the workshop time slot.

Remote Participation

  • Oral presentations: remote presentation via Zoom is supported.
  • Poster presentations: virtual posters are not supported. Remote authors may present via a short-format Zoom presentation instead.
  • The Zoom link will be shared with registered remote participants only.

Registration

  • Participants must register for each workshop they attend.
  • Remote attendance is available at the remote registration rate.
  • In-person badges reflect all purchased items; registration is required for on-site access.
