About SPEAKABLE 2026

SPEAKABLE 2026 is a research workshop dedicated to speech, language, and multimodal technologies, with a special focus on low-resource and under-represented settings. The workshop brings together researchers and practitioners working on speech LLMs, speech technologies, and multimodal models.

The workshop will feature invited talks, paper presentations, and interactive discussions on data resources, model architectures, evaluation methods, and ethical aspects of speech-driven AI.

Call for Papers (CFP)

We invite original, unpublished work on topics including but not limited to:

  • Speech-centric large language models and speech foundation models
  • Multimodal models combining speech, text, vision, and other modalities
  • Low-resource and endangered languages for speech and multimodal AI
  • Datasets, benchmarks, and evaluation for speech LLMs
  • Clinical, educational, and societal applications of speech technologies
  • Ethical, legal, and social implications of speech-driven AI systems

Submission guidelines, paper format, and the link to the submission system will be published here.
